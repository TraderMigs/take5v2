Free Pickleball Mini-Game

Take5 Pickleball Break is built for a quick reset. Start the game, track the incoming ball, charge your swing, and return the shot over the net.

Find Pickleball Courts Near You

Use the court finder inside the net to search for pickleball courts, open play, clubs, leagues, lessons, and games near your current city.

How to Play

Move the floating paddle hand toward the yellow pickleball. Charge your hit with click or spacebar. A weak shot hits the net. A clean shot clears it. Too much power sends it out.

Why Take Five?

Sometimes you need a simple break that does not feel like work. This mini-game is designed to be fast, clean, simple, and easy to share.
